SMS Terms & Privacy Policy

This page outlines the SMS communication practices for Software Velocity Partners and our applications, including Roundly (Looper Golf).

Program Description: We send transactional alerts, account notifications, and golf round updates to users who have opted-in to receive SMS through our application.

1. SMS Terms of Service

By providing your mobile number and opting in to receive messages, you agree to the following:

  • Message Frequency: Message frequency varies based on user activity (e.g., scoring updates, account verification).
  • Opt-Out: You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text "STOP" to +1 844-703-3190. After you send the SMS message "STOP" to us, we will send you an SMS message to confirm that you have been unsubscribed. After this, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us.
  • Help: If you are experiencing issues with the messaging program you can reply with the keyword HELP for more assistance, or you can get help directly at support@softwarevelocitypartners.com.
  • Rates: Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. As always, message and data rates may apply for any messages sent to you from us and to us from you.

2. Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. Your mobile information is handled with the following protections:

  • No Third-Party Sharing: Mobile information will not be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.
  • Data Use: We use your phone number exclusively for the functional operation of the application (e.g., authentication, transactional notifications).

3. How Users Opt-In

Users opt-in by entering their mobile number within the application settings or during the registration process. Below is an example of the consent language shown to users:

"By providing your phone number, you agree to receive text messages from Roundly for account notifications. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to opt-out, HELP for help."
